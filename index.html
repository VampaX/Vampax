Who Am I?

After Getting my Degree of Electronics and Communication Engineer from Uttar Pradesh Technical University in 2014, I joined Times World Group, New Delhi as Research Engineer. My first job profile made me curious towards all latest technical developments and new possibilities in the section of IT & Electronics Industries. I was blessed with great team which made so many projects possible related to Home and Industrial Automation Apps, Embedded Devices and IOT Implementations for different industries. These first two years in tech world were filled with rapid succession of ups and downs, and plenty of hard-won lessons.

These lessons proved to be milestone as I was welcomed at leading institutes like IIT delhi, IIT Roorkee, NIT Trichy and other leading universities and colleges of the country for delivering industrial trainings and talks. Apart from this some leading IT corporates, Govt. Agencies like Indian Navy, National Security Agengies and NSDC used some valuable inputs and suggestions for their real time projects. With Revert Technologies Pvt. Ltd, Gurugram, I had enlightened engineering students and corporate guys through some really informative technical talks and trainings based upon Artificial Intelligence, IOT and Robotics. Leading a technical team with SkillIT Nation made me understand the values of creating industry oriented technical training contents and building team with productive co-operation among them.

With a count of 7000+ trained technical students and 1500+ academician and working professionals motivates me alot towards continuous research and subsequent sharing of the outcomes.
